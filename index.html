Frequently Asked Questions

Established in 2000, Gold Standard Buys is the most trusted buyer of diamonds, fine jewelry and luxury watches in the world. We are passionate about quality, sustainability, and the valuation of finely crafted jewels. We strive to honor each in everything we do. Whether selling or buying, our clients receive unparalleled expertise and discretion. With experts in 21 locations around the globe and access to the latest market data, we offer a seamless process, incomparable value, and immediate payment.

An appointment with Gold Standard Buys is a luxurious experience. On the day of your appointment, one of our experts will examine the jewelry or watches you’re interested in selling. There’s no need to take notes. We do that for you, writing descriptions of your pieces and our offer for you to see. You may have questions about your jewelry and our offer. That’s okay. We’re here to help and answer any questions you may have. Should you accept our offer, we'll pay you by bank transfer right then and there.

Gold Standard Buys buyers take many factors into consideration when offering for your diamonds, jewelry and watches. Diamonds are evaluated based on their inherit characteristics, including the 4C’s (Carat, Cut, Color, Clarity), overall appearance and beauty of the stone, as well as current demand for those attributes. When we make offers for your jewelry, we account for the maker, the vintage, the condition, quality, style and desirability of each piece. With our network of enthusiastic international jewelry collectors, we can ensure that our prices are competitive. When offering for your watch, condition, vintage, and market demand are just as important as it is for jewelry. And of course, the brand of your watch makes all the difference. Having original paperwork and boxes prove helpful, but are not necessary, for us to make an offer.
